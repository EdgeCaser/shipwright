Full autonomy is unacceptable because pricing, commitments, and operational exceptions carry financial and reputational risk.\n\n## Goal\nReduce manual coordination time for inbound event workflows while preserving operator control over commitments, pricing, and exception handling.\n\n## Non-Goals\n- Fully autonomous quote issuance\n- Autonomous contract negotiation\n- Autonomous payment collection or refunds\n- Open-ended LLM action execution against production systems\n- Multi-channel support beyond WhatsApp in Phase 1\n\n## Target Users\n- Event requester communicating over WhatsApp\n- Internal operations agent managing requests and approvals\n- Manager or pricing approver responsible for quote commitments\n\n## Jobs To Be Done\n- As an event requester, I want fast answers and clear next steps over WhatsApp.\n- As an ops agent, I want inbound requests converted into structured cases with suggested replies and draft pricing.\n- As a pricing approver, I want to review and explicitly approve any monetary commitment before it is sent.\n\n## Phase 1 Scope\n### In Scope\n- WhatsApp assistant for inbound and outbound customer messaging\n- Conversation-to-case creation with structured fields\n- Internal console for case review, state changes, reply drafting, and approvals\n- Deterministic workflow engine for routing, gating, and audit logging\n- LLM usage limited to bounded tasks such as summarization, extraction, intent classification, and response drafting\n- Human approval checkpoint for all pricing commitments before external send\n- Full audit trail of model outputs, approvals, edits, and outbound messages\n\n### Out of Scope\n- Auto-sending final quotes without approval\n- Contract drafting beyond template fill assistance\n- Voice calls\n- Deep CRM or ERP bidirectional sync unless needed for core case context\n\n## Product Principles\n1. Deterministic systems own state, policy, and side effects.\n2. LLMs may suggest, summarize, classify, or draft, but may not commit money, policy, or workflow transitions without explicit rules.\n3. Every external commitment must be attributable to a human or a deterministic rule approved in advance.\n4. message summarization\n  - intent classification\n  - field extraction into predefined schema\n  - draft reply generation from approved templates and case context\n- LLM must not:\n  - set or approve price\n  - System proposes pricing draft or pricing inputs from deterministic rules/calculator.\n3. Draft outbound pricing message is generated.\n4. Case enters approval queue.\n5. Human approver approves or edits.\n6. Deterministic send service delivers approved message to WhatsApp.\n\n### Workflow C: Non-Pricing Follow-Up\n1. Customer asks operational question.\n2. System classifies request.\n3. Draft reply is generated from approved knowledge/template sources.\n4.
