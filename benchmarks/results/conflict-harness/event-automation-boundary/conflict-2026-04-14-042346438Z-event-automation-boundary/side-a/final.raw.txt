Scope\n- Dynamic pricing optimization.\n- Auto-negotiation.\n- Fully automated exception handling.\n- Direct write access into downstream ERP/accounting systems.\n\n## Core User Flow\n1. Customer sends an inquiry in WhatsApp.\n2. System captures the message and creates or links a lead/event thread.\n3. Deterministic preprocessing runs.\n4. LLM may extract structured fields, classify inquiry type, summarize context, and draft missing follow-up questions.\n5. Workflow rules validate required fields and either send approved deterministic follow-up questions or route the thread for operator review.\n6. Operator reviews the conversation, corrects extracted fields, and prepares a response.\n7. If the response contains pricing or another approval-triggering commitment, the system blocks send until an authorized human explicitly approves it.\n8. The approved outbound message is sent through WhatsApp with timestamped attribution and an audit log of draft, edits, approval, and final content.\n\n## Functional Requirements\n### WhatsApp internal user.\n- Rejected drafts must return to editable state with reason capture.\n- The approval control may be implemented as a queue or as an in-thread approval state, provided the gate is explicit, auditable, and role-restricted.\n\n### Deterministic Boundary Layer\n- All external actions must be triggered by deterministic business rules, not free-form model decisions.\n- LLM outputs
