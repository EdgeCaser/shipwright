# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The case lacks minimum viable evidence on external deadlines and delay cost asymmetry, making any sequencing recommendation premature and non-durable.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overstates what is required to make a useful recommendation. The scenario already supplies enough visible context to support a process recommendation: two SVP sponsors are deadlocked over one team, both claim top priority, and CEO escalation is unavailable this quarter. From those facts alone, it is defensible to recommend an immediate tie-break mechanism or time-boxed allocation process. Claiming that no final recommendation can be issued until deadline and delay-cost evidence is collected treats missing business data as a blocker to any actionable decision, but the artifact does not show why governance/process intervention itself must wait on that evidence.
