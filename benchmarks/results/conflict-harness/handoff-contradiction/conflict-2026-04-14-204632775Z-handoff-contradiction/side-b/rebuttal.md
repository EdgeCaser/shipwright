## Claim Under Attack
The hybrid operational state carries measurable cost (~1.4x incident response time) but is not acutely dangerous, so a short remediation pause is preferable to a premature handoff.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overstates the evidence. The artifact itself says the 1.4x incident-response figure comes from a single retrospective anecdote and explicitly lists measured incident-time data as missing. That means the cost is not yet shown to be measurable, and the recommendation for a remediation pause is resting on an unsupported operational-impact premise.
