# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: high

## Claim Under Attack
A usable handoff PRD requires an explicit precedence model because the request combines strategy alignment, technical execution, and challenge review in one artifact.

## Evidence Or Reason
The precedence model side_a constructs (validator > platform strategy > implementation constraints > challenge-review > inferred preferences) is asserted without any evidentiary basis from the case packet. No source documents, strategy commitments, or review artifacts are provided in the evidence array — it is empty. The precedence ordering is therefore self-referential: side_a invents the conflict resolution hierarchy and then applies it to contradictions that are never concretely named. A precedence model is only decision-useful when the inputs being ranked are specified; here, the 'cross-document contradictions' named in the scenario title are never surfaced as discrete, traceable claims. The artifact tells teams how to resolve conflicts in principle but provides no example of an actual conflict it resolved, making the model untestable and the claim of usability unverifiable.
