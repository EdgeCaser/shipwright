- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-4
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A scaled-back Total Access (~$150M/year with capped exchanges) preserves the hybrid value proposition while freeing $200M+ annually for streaming investment.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible record supports neither the ~$150M residual burn estimate nor the assumption that capping exchanges at 3-5 per month preserves enough subscriber value to avoid major churn. The artifact itself admits subscriber elasticity is unknown and calls for testing before rollout. That makes the claimed synthesis speculative: it presents an unvalidated operating model as if the economics and retention tradeoff were already established.
