## Claim Under Attack
The organizational drag problem that motivates separation is solvable through internal P&L and leadership restructuring without requiring customers to manage two separate accounts, billing relationships, and queues.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim asserts a feasible alternative without visible supporting evidence on system architecture, operating-model constraints, or whether separate incentives can actually be created while keeping a unified customer experience. The artifact itself concedes that the degree of DVD/streaming technical and operational entanglement is unknown. Without evidence that internal-only separation can deliver the same strategic benefits on an acceptable timeline, the recommendation rests on an unproven substitute rather than a demonstrated option.
