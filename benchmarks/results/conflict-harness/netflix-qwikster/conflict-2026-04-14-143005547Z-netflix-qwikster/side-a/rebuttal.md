- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The operational benefits of separation can be fully captured through internal divisional reorganization without customer-facing brand bifurcation.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overreaches beyond the visible evidence. The case packet supports that bundling creates organizational drag, but it does not show that an internal reorg would fully reproduce the incentives, accountability, capital allocation discipline, and strategic clarity of a true split. 'Fully captured' is the key unsupported leap: no evidence is offered that separate P&Ls and teams inside one brand would prevent cross-subsidization, resolve roadmap conflicts, or avoid management attention being pulled back to DVD economics. The artifact treats an asserted alternative as proven equivalent without evidence.
