# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The Qwikster split destroys customer value (separate logins, queues, reviews) without delivering any customer benefit, and the organizational benefits it seeks are achievable through internal restructuring without brand separation.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible evidence supports the customer-friction half of this claim, but not the stronger conclusion that the split delivers no customer benefit and that all strategic benefits are fully achievable internally. The case packet says bundling creates organizational drag and that separation would let each business optimize independently; the artifact does not provide evidence quantifying that drag, showing internal restructuring was feasible in 2011, or proving a unified brand could preserve the same strategic clarity for investors, content partners, and operating teams. As written, the claim overreaches beyond the evidence shown.
