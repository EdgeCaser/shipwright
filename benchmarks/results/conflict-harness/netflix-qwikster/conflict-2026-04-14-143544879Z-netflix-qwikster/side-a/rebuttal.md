# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Internal organizational separation (separate BUs, teams, P&L) achieves the strategic focus benefits of Qwikster without the brand destruction, customer friction, or financial exposure of a full corporate split.

## Evidence Or Reason
This is asserted, not demonstrated. The artifact offers no visible evidence that internal BUs would actually remove the core constraints separation is meant to solve: capital-allocation conflicts, leadership attention split, legacy-DVD incentives distorting streaming decisions, and the lack of clean external accountability for a declining cash cow versus a loss-making growth business. The Amazon analogy is not evidence for Netflix's specific governance problem in September 2011. Without proof that internal separation delivers comparable decision speed and strategic clarity, the claim overstates a hypothetical alternative as if it were established fact.
