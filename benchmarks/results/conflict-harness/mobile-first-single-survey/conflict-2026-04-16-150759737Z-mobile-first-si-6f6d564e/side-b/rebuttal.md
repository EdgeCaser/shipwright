# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Power users are a systematically biased sample for mobile pain assessment because they are more likely to use desktop for complex tasks and may express mobile preference without behavioral follow-through.

## Evidence Or Reason
The case packet provides no evidence that these power users are desktop-skewed, that their workflows are unusually complex, or that their stated mobile pain lacks behavioral follow-through. Those may be plausible hypotheses, but the artifact presents them as if they are grounded in known user behavior when no behavioral data exists at all. That weakens the claim because it relies on an unverified stereotype about power users rather than visible evidence from the scenario.
