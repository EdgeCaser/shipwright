# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The single NPS survey of 50 power users is insufficient to justify a 6-month full redesign commitment; behavioral corroboration and broader sample validation are required before engineering work begins.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact never shows that behavioral corroboration and broader-sample validation are necessary preconditions rather than preferred diligence steps. From the visible record, the survey is weak evidence for a full 6-month commitment, but the stronger claim that no engineering work should begin without those specific validations is unsupported. A team could reasonably start lower-cost design exploration, prototyping, or scoped mobile fixes based on the existing signal while gathering more evidence in parallel. The visible evidence supports caution, not this hard necessity standard.
