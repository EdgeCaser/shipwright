# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Yahoo's display advertising dominance is under structural threat from Facebook's rapid 2008 user growth, which fragments attention away from portal display toward social platforms with superior targeting data.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible evidence only establishes that Yahoo is #1 in US display and that Facebook grew from 100M to 200M users in 2008. It does not show that Facebook had already translated user growth into material display spend capture from Yahoo, nor that Yahoo's display properties were in near-term decline severe enough to overturn the standalone case in May 2008. The claim also adds an unproven step about 'superior targeting data' and assumes portal inventory is commoditizing without citing case-packet evidence on Yahoo display revenue erosion, RPM decline, or advertiser migration rates.
