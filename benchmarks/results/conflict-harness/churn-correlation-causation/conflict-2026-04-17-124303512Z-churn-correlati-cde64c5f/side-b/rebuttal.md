# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A broad 30-day non-use targeting campaign should not be funded before a randomized pilot establishes both that adoption can be driven and that driven adoption produces retention lift.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overreaches beyond the visible evidence. The packet shows only that causation is unproven; it does not establish that no campaign work should be funded before a randomized pilot reads out. A reasonable alternative is a constrained, explicitly non-causal PRD that funds instrumentation, segmentation, and a low-risk pilot rollout rather than a broad retention commitment. The artifact does not provide evidence that every pre-pilot investment is wasteful, only that full causal confidence is absent.
