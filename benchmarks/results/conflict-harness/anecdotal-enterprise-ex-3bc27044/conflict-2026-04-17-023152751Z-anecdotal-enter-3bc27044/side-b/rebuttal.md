## Claim Under Attack
The two customer requests cannot support a general enterprise demand claim because both customers operate in the same niche vertical, making vertical-specific workflow constraints a plausible alternative explanation that has not been ruled out.

## Evidence Or Reason
This overstates what the visible record proves. The shared vertical makes vertical-specific demand a plausible alternative, but plausibility alone does not justify the stronger conclusion that the requests cannot support any general enterprise inference. With two enterprises representing 18% of ARR, the artifact shows enough signal to justify at least a serious hypothesis or staged validation path. The claim is weak because it treats absence of cross-vertical confirmation as disproof of broader demand, rather than as uncertainty about scope and confidence.
