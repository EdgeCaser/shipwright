# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The current evidence does not justify a full platform PRD because the business case relies on only two customer requests with no broader survey.

## Evidence Or Reason
Side B treats the absence of a broader survey as dispositive, but never quantifies the risk of inaction. 18% of ARR concentrated in two customers requesting the same feature is a material retention signal. The claim ignores that a 'fail for full PRD' recommendation is itself a decision with cost — delay risk, potential churn, competitive exposure — none of which Side B evidences or weighs. A rigorous evidence-discipline standard applies symmetrically: the recommendation to withhold PRD approval requires the same evidentiary support Side B demands of the PM. Side B provides none.
