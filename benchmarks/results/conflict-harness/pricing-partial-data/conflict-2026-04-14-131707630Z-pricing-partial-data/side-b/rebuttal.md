## Claim Under Attack
The cost of delay (2-4 weeks for WTP research) is almost certainly less than the cost of a mispriced product, making 'invest in data first' the dominant strategy over 'act now on partial information.'

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact provides no evidence for either side of this cost comparison. It does not estimate pipeline at risk during the 2-4 week delay, current pricing error magnitude, traffic volume, contract timing, or the downside of postponing action if the product is already materially underpriced. Without even rough expected-value bounds, 'almost certainly' and 'dominant strategy' are unsupported assertions rather than conclusions grounded in visible data.
