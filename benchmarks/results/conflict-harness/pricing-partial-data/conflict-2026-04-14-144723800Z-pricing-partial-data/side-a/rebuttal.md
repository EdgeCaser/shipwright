# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A staged A/B pricing experiment with pre-defined kill criteria can generate the missing WTP and elasticity data while limiting blast radius, converting the partial-data problem into a solvable one within 1-2 quarters.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overstates what the proposed experiment can establish under the visible facts. No evidence is provided that pricing can be cleanly randomized, that sales can maintain test/control discipline, or that volume is sufficient to infer elasticity within 6-8 weeks. The artifact also conflates observed conversion/churn effects with true willingness-to-pay; a limited field test may show response to a specific offer, not recover segment-level WTP curves. Without proof of operational feasibility and statistical power, the claim that the experiment both limits blast radius and resolves the core data gap within 1-2 quarters is unsupported.
