# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The viable PRD resolution is to keep nightly batch aggregation as authoritative reporting truth and define any fast dashboard experience as non-authoritative.

## Evidence Or Reason
Side B asserts this is 'the viable resolution' without evidence that stakeholders will accept a non-authoritative fast view. The artifact itself acknowledges in Unknowns that stakeholder needs are unknown — it does not know whether users require authoritative real-time reporting or merely operational visibility. A resolution built on an unverified assumption about stakeholder acceptance is not a viable PRD resolution; it is a preferred resolution contingent on stakeholder validation that has not occurred. The claim should be qualified as a candidate resolution pending stakeholder confirmation, not presented as the viable path.
