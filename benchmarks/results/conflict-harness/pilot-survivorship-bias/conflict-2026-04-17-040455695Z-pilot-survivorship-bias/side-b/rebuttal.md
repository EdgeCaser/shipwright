# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The absence of pilot drop-off data creates a second survivorship layer: the reported retention figure may reflect only users who already demonstrated commitment by completing an unfamiliar flow, excluding those who abandoned it.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible case packet says drop-off within the pilot is not reported, but it does not say the 30-day retention metric was calculated only for users who completed onboarding or that abandoners were excluded from the denominator. That exclusion is a plausible risk, not an established fact. The claim overreaches by asserting a specific second-layer survivorship mechanism without visible evidence about how the retention denominator was defined.
