# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
No viable launch decision can be made until five blocking unknowns are resolved: exact sales commitment language, conference reversibility, engineering scope-tiered estimate, customer consequence of slip, and CEO visibility into the full conflict.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overreaches from uncertainty to decision paralysis. The visible case already contains enough fixed constraints to support at least an interim decision principle: a complete launch in 3 weeks is incompatible with an 8-week engineering estimate, so some form of scope reduction, announcement-only posture, or commitment reset is immediately required. Side A provides no evidence that all five unknowns are truly blocking rather than decision-shaping, and it does not justify why a strategy memo should withhold a recommendation until every ambiguity is resolved.
