# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A full launch in 3 weeks should not be committed because the stated engineering timeline, sales commitments, and conference timing cannot all be satisfied simultaneously as presented.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim treats the 8-week engineering estimate as a fixed constraint without interrogating its basis. Engineering estimates in pre-launch scenarios routinely compress under scoped requirements or additional resourcing. The memo never asks whether the 3-week gap could be closed by narrowing launch scope (e.g., limited GA, beta with key accounts, or a feature-flagged release), adding engineering capacity, or deferring non-critical features. Without establishing what the 8-week estimate actually covers and whether scope reduction is off the table, the incompatibility assertion is asserted rather than demonstrated. The recommendation to 'not commit' rests on an unexamined premise.
