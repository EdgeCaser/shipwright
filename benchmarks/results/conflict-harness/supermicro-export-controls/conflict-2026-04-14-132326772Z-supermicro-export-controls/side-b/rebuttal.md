## Claim Under Attack
A targeted pause in high-risk channels preserves the majority of revenue (domestic hyperscaler and allied-country business continues) while eliminating the most dangerous exposure vectors.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact provides no revenue mix, channel exposure, or customer concentration evidence to support the assertion that 'the majority of revenue' sits safely outside the paused channels. Its own open questions admit the board does not yet know what percentage of revenue touches restricted geographies. Without that mapping, the claim that a targeted pause is financially bounded is speculative, and the recommendation relies on an unproven assumption about how much business can continue unaffected.
