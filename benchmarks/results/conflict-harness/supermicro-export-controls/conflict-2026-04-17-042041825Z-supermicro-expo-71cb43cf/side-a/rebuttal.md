## Claim Under Attack
The board should pause and narrow high-risk activity until the independent investigation and compliance reset are complete.

## Evidence Or Reason
Side B never defines what 'high-risk activity' means operationally. Without a concrete taxonomy of which channels, geographies, or transaction types qualify, 'pause high-risk activity' is not an actionable board decision — it is a restatement of the problem. The case packet confirms Supermicro is in intense AI-infrastructure demand; an undefined pause could de facto become a broad freeze if management cannot draw the line, handing share to rivals without any compliance benefit. Side B acknowledges this execution risk in its Pass/Fail section but provides no criteria to resolve it, leaving the core recommendation operationally hollow.
