# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The board faces asymmetric downside risk: the cost of underreacting (entity-level enforcement, Nvidia relationship damage, governance crisis) vastly exceeds the cost of a targeted, time-bounded compliance pause.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact asserts a strong asymmetry without quantifying either side of the tradeoff. The visible record shows an investigation is underway, the company is not named as a defendant, and investor concern exists, but it does not establish probability or magnitude for entity-level enforcement, Nvidia action, or governance collapse. On the other side, the memo itself admits unknowns about revenue concentration in high-risk channels, customer contract exposure, and switching risk. Without those facts, 'vastly exceeds' is an unsupported leap rather than a demonstrated conclusion.
