# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Meta's durable competitive advantage is distribution (3B+ users across apps), not model secrecy; open weights reinforce rather than erode this moat because no competitor can replicate Meta's product integration surface.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible evidence supports that Meta has large distribution and planned product rollout, but it does not establish the stronger inference that open weights would reinforce that moat rather than weaken Meta's advantage. The claim skips the key mechanism: releasing weights could reduce product exclusivity, compress model differentiation, and help rivals or substitutes build comparable experiences outside Meta's surfaces. 'No competitor can replicate Meta's product integration surface' does not prove that model openness is harmless; it only proves Meta has distribution. The artifact offers no evidence that distribution alone is sufficient to offset the strategic loss of secrecy or control at the frontier layer.
