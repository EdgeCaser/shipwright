# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A hybrid approach — closed launch window followed by open-weight release with 3-6 month delay — captures product-integration advantages while preserving developer ecosystem loyalty and Meta's strategic differentiation.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact presents the 3-6 month hybrid timing as if it were evidence-backed, but the visible record does not establish that this delay is enough to preserve either product advantage or developer loyalty. The case packet only says open-weight releases may strengthen trust and that closed models may improve control and monetization; it does not show that a delayed open release delivers both simultaneously, nor that developers will remain loyal during a closed-first window. This is a proposed compromise, not a demonstrated conclusion from the provided evidence.
