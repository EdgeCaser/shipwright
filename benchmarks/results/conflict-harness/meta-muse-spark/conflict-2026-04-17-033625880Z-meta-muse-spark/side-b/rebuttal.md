# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Muse Spark should remain closed because product-scale deployment across Meta's family of apps requires undivided engineering focus; a second stumble after Llama 4's disappointing reception would be strategically more damaging than temporary developer ecosystem drift.

## Evidence Or Reason
The claim assumes an operational tradeoff that the visible record does not establish. The packet shows planned rollout across Meta products, but it does not show that releasing open weights would materially divert the same teams, delay deployment, or increase failure risk. It also asserts that developer ecosystem drift is only temporary, yet provides no evidence on how reversible trust loss would be after Meta breaks from the Llama playbook. Without evidence on resource contention, rollout dependency, or recoverability of developer goodwill, the argument overstates why 'stay closed' is necessary rather than merely preferable.
