# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The product-integration rationale for closure is only a 60-90 day timing issue, not a valid longer-term strategy, because Meta's moat comes from distribution and integration rather than weight secrecy.

## Evidence Or Reason
The 60-90 day limit is asserted without support in the case packet. The visible evidence says Muse Spark is rolling out over the coming weeks across multiple Meta surfaces and that larger Muse-family models are in development, but it does not show that product advantage is fully secured after one quarter or that open weights would not materially weaken API leverage, model secrecy, safety control, or competitor catch-up beyond that window. This claim depends on an unproven assumption about how long integration advantages last versus how durable frontier-model secrecy remains.
