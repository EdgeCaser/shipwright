# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Meta should adopt a tiered open strategy: keep Muse Spark closed for 90-120 days to secure product integration and partner leverage, then release open weights while retaining a proprietary integration layer.

## Evidence Or Reason
The core prescription depends on a precise 90-120 day delay and on the feasibility of separating open weights from a durable proprietary integration layer, but the artifact provides no visible evidence for either. The case packet confirms rollout is planned over the coming weeks and that private-preview API access exists, yet it does not show that four months is the decision-relevant window, that partner leverage materially matures inside that period, or that Meta can preserve a meaningful moat once weights are released. This makes the proposed middle path look invented rather than derived from the available evidence.
