- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The appropriate PRD outcome is a discovery-gated prototype phase with explicit success thresholds rather than a production commitment.

## Evidence Or Reason
The success thresholds proposed (5 users complete evaluation, 3 report problem as frequent, 3 prefer this approach) are presented as explicit and rigorous, but they are themselves arbitrary numbers with no evidence justifying why these specific thresholds constitute a meaningful signal. A sample of 5 users is too small to distinguish real demand from selection bias or courtesy agreement. The artifact criticizes the feature for lacking evidence while simultaneously proposing pass/fail gates built on equally unsupported assumptions about what constitutes adequate validation. The thresholds create an illusion of disciplined gating without any statistical or methodological basis for the cutoffs chosen.
