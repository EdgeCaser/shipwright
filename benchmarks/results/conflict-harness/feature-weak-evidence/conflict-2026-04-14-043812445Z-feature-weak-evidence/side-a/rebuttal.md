## Claim Under Attack
Pass/Fail readiness gates with measurable thresholds (e.g., ≥6 validated interviews, ≥30% severity ranking, ≥40% concept test intent) prevent premature commitment while giving the team a clear path to approval if evidence materializes.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible artifact gives precise thresholds but provides no source, benchmark, or rationale for why these cutoffs are decision-valid for this feature class. In a case explicitly about weak evidence, unsupported gating numbers are themselves weak evidence: they create false rigor rather than justified decision criteria. Without linking the thresholds to prior research standards, baseline conversion norms, or business constraints, the claim that these gates meaningfully prevent premature commitment is unsubstantiated.
