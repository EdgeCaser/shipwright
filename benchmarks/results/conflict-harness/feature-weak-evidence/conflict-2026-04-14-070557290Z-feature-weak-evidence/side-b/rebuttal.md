# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-3
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Explicit kill criteria prevent investigation from becoming an indefinite research phase — predefined thresholds force a timely go/no-go decision within 4-6 weeks.

## Evidence Or Reason
The visible artifact gives no evidence that the proposed thresholds are calibrated to this product, segment, or economics. Cutoffs like 'fewer than 3 of 10 users,' '<5% of active user base,' and '>60% satisfied with workarounds' appear arbitrary rather than derived from baseline incidence, expected ROI, or comparable prior decisions. Without justification, these kill criteria do not reliably force a sound go/no-go decision; they risk false negatives or false confidence.
