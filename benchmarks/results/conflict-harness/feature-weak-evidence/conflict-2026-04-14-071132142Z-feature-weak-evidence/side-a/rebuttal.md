## Claim Under Attack
A 2-week Discovery Research Brief is the correct next artifact because it is the cheapest way to determine whether the feature idea has any evidentiary basis, requiring only PM/researcher time and no engineering resources.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim asserts cost, duration, and resource needs without any supporting evidence from the case packet or artifact. The artifact itself admits the feature is unspecified and that no first-party data or technical assessment has been performed, so it cannot credibly conclude that a 2-week brief is the cheapest valid path or that no engineering input is needed. For some feature classes, a quick engineering spike or data pull could be cheaper and more informative than interviews plus competitive research.
