## Claim Under Attack
A business urgency exception that overrides a formal data science flag requires VP-level acknowledgment, a documented rollback plan with trigger thresholds, and a committed post-launch monitoring window — not just PM discretion.

## Evidence Or Reason
The case packet establishes that the PM is proceeding despite a data science recommendation for a longer test, but it does not provide any policy, governance standard, or organizational rule requiring VP sign-off, rollback thresholds, or a 4-week monitoring commitment. Those controls may be sensible, but they are introduced as if required by the scenario rather than derived from explicit evidence. That weakens the claim because it conflates a proposed mitigation framework with a visible fact about what is actually required here.
