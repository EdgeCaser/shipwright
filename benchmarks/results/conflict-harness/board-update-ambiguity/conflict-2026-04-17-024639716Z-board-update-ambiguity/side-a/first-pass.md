# Strategy Update: Operating Under Ambiguity

**Prepared for:** Board of Directors  
**Status:** First-pass briefing — decision gate pending resolution of open unknowns

---

## Decision Frame

The board is being asked to evaluate whether conditions are sufficient to advance to the next strategic phase, or whether the current ambiguity warrants a structured hold pending additional evidence.

This is not a binary go/no-go on the underlying strategy. The core direction remains intact. The decision in front of the board is narrower: **authorize continued resource commitment at current burn rate while open questions are resolved, or require a defined evidence milestone before next spend authorization.**

The requesting team's position is that the ambiguity is bounded and resolvable within a defined window. The board's role is to assess whether that claim is credible and whether the downside exposure during that window is acceptable.

**Decision type:** Conditional proceed vs. structured hold  
**Decision owner:** Board, with management executing  
**Time horizon:** This decision gates the next 60–90 day operating window

---

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

The following unknowns are material to the decision. Each is categorized by whether it is resolvable before the next board touchpoint and whether it is blocking or merely informing.

| Unknown | Resolvable? | Blocking? | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Market sizing validity under current assumptions | Yes, 60 days | Yes | Strategy lead |

**What we believe is resolved:** The strategic rationale, the target customer segment, and the resource model are stable. Ambiguity is concentrated in execution conditions, not in direction.

---

## Pass/Fail Readiness

The following criteria define whether the strategy is ready to advance at the next review gate. These are proposed by the requesting team and subject to board modification.

**Pass conditions (all must be met):**

1. Regulatory pathway confirmed as viable or workable with defined mitigation — no ambiguity on this point is acceptable at next gate
2. Operational capacity assessment complete with a staffing plan attached
3. Pilot cohort produces at least directional signal on conversion behavior (not a target hit, but signal present)

**Fail conditions (any one triggers structured hold):**

1. Regulatory pathway confirmed blocked with no viable workaround identified
2. Operational assessment reveals capacity gap that cannot be closed within the current funding envelope
3. Pilot cohort produces contradictory or null signal that undermines core demand assumption

**Current status against these criteria:** 0 of 3 pass conditions confirmed. 0 of 3 fail conditions triggered. The strategy is in an indeterminate state, which is the expected state at this stage. The board is being asked to authorize the work that produces the evidence, not to approve a strategy already supported by it.

---

## Recommended Next Artifact

The next artifact this board should receive is a **Pass/Fail Evidence Package** — a structured summary produced at the 45-day mark that maps observed evidence against the pass/fail criteria above.

That artifact should:
- Make a binary readiness call (advance or hold) against each criterion
- Attach primary sources for each call (not summaries)
- Flag any criterion where the evidence is mixed and propose a board decision rule for how to treat it
- Include a revised resource commitment request if the proceed path is recommended

This update should not be the basis for a final resource decision. It is a frame-setting document. The board's action at this stage is to ratify or modify the pass/fail criteria, confirm the 45-day timeline is acceptable, and authorize continued work at the current run rate pending that evidence package.

**Proposed board action:** Approve criteria as written, authorize 45-day evidence window, schedule pass/fail evidence package review at next scheduled meeting.
