# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The organization is not ready for a go/no-go commitment because the four critical unknowns (WTP, competitive position, unit economics, regulatory trajectory) remain at directional-confidence level, not evidentiary-confidence level.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim asserts that all four unknowns are decision-critical and collectively sufficient to block a go/no-go decision, but the artifact provides no underlying evidence refs, no scenario facts, and no basis for why these are the decisive gating variables versus a narrower subset. Several thresholds are also invented without support, so the conclusion that the organization is not ready reads as plausible framing rather than evidence-backed diagnosis.
