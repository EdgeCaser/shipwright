# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-2
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
A two-track approach (immediate intervention on aligned signals, instrumentation before full program) is the correct response to conflicting signals because it avoids both analysis paralysis and uninformed action.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact does not establish that any aligned-signal segment actually exists or that the immediate interventions are evidence-backed. Its only quantified basis for Track A is an admitted placeholder estimate of 35-45% of churn volume, and it explicitly excludes specific intervention designs, technical implementation details, and success metrics for Track A. That makes the proposed immediate intervention track vulnerable to the same uninformed-action risk it claims to avoid.
