- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The organization cannot commit to either churn intervention or growth-first strategy because critical segment-level data is missing and the conflict is unresolvable without knowing where churn is concentrated.

## Evidence Or Reason
This overstates what the visible artifact supports. The artifact already shows multiple non-segmented but convergent warning signals: declining 90-day cohort retention, a 2.1x spike in reliability tickets, and consistent NPS complaints tied to the v4.2 migration. Those signals may be insufficient to pick a fully targeted intervention, but they are enough to justify some immediate low-regret retention action while analysis continues. Claiming the decision is unresolvable without segment cuts sets up a false binary between perfect diagnosis and no commitment.
