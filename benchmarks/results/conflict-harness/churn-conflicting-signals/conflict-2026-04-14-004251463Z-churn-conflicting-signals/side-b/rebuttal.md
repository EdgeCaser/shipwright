## Claim Under Attack
Parallel-track approach (signal clarification + low-risk interventions) is the optimal strategy when signals conflict: full commitment risks misallocation while pure analysis risks delayed response to a worsening problem.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact asserts the parallel-track approach is optimal and that Track 2 interventions are low-risk and valuable under all signal interpretations, but it does not substantiate the cost-of-delay, intervention cost, opportunity cost, or failure modes. Health scoring and CSM playbooks can create alert fatigue, misprioritize accounts, and consume scarce analytics/CX capacity before segmentation is known, so the claim needs evidence or a decision threshold rather than assuming these interventions are universally low-risk.
