# Critique: finding-2

- Target Side: side_a
- Target Claim IDs: side_a-claim-4
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
Onboarding funnel instrumentation is the one intervention safe to ship before diagnosis is complete because it serves both growth and retention goals and produces actionable data regardless of root cause.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact does not justify the word 'safe.' It states onboarding time-to-value is lengthening, but provides no visible evidence that onboarding is a top churn driver versus pricing, competitive displacement, support quality, or customer mix. It also concedes instrumentation needs 2 weeks to implement and 4 weeks for meaningful data, while engineering capacity is already constrained. Without showing implementation cost, opportunity cost, or why instrumentation outranks other low-regret actions, the claim overreaches from 'plausibly useful' to 'the one safe intervention.'
