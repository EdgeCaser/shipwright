# Critique: finding-1

- Target Side: side_b
- Target Claim IDs: side_b-claim-1
- Attack Type: evidence_gap
- Severity: medium

## Claim Under Attack
The PRD is not ready for engineering investment because no evidence validates that handoff failures are the primary driver of conversation reopens — building without this risks solving a secondary cause.

## Evidence Or Reason
This claim overreaches beyond the visible artifact. The artifact provides no evidence that conversation reopens are the sole or required decision metric for a handoff-improvement PRD, and it introduces an unsupported gating standard that handoff-related reopens must be the primary driver. A workflow-improvement PRD could still be decision-useful if handoff failures are a meaningful but non-primary source of delay, rework, or ownership ambiguity. Without evidence tying readiness exclusively to reopen causality, the 'not ready for engineering investment' conclusion is asserted rather than demonstrated.
