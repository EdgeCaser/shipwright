## Claim Under Attack
Delay costs approximately $50-100M per month in additional exposure from carrying costs and price deterioration risk on 7,000 underwater homes, making speed of decision a first-order strategic concern.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact gives no calculation linking the cited facts to a $50-100M monthly loss range. The case packet provides a total estimated write-down exposure of $300-500M, but not monthly carrying costs, expected monthly home-price decline, average inventory value, financing expense, or time-to-disposition assumptions. Without those inputs, the monthly burn figure is asserted rather than demonstrated, so it overstates precision on a key urgency claim.
