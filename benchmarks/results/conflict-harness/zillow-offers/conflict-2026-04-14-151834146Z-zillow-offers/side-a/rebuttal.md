## Claim Under Attack
Exiting Offers would catalyze a re-rating of Zillow stock as a high-margin marketplace/SaaS business rather than a loss-making real estate speculation operation, unlocking the value currently obscured by blended financials.

## Evidence Or Reason
The artifact shows that Offers is masking core business quality, but it does not provide evidence that exit would actually produce a valuation re-rating, let alone a material one. The cited facts only establish current stock decline and IMT profitability; they do not isolate how much of the decline is attributable to Offers versus broader housing-tech multiple compression, execution credibility damage, liquidation losses, or restructuring overhang. A rerating claim needs comparative valuation logic, investor reaction evidence, or scenario analysis, none of which is visible here.
