# Strategic Recommendation: Supermicro Export-Controls Response

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Pause selected high-risk expansion channels for one quarter and complete a visible compliance reset before returning to full-speed growth.

The AI server opportunity is real, but a governance failure in an export-controls context can destroy strategic degrees of freedom faster than short-term revenue can restore them.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Whether the alleged misconduct was isolated or exposed broader control weaknesses
- Whether Nvidia or large customers will quietly change allocation behavior
- How severe any additional regulatory action could become
- How much revenue risk comes from narrowing high-risk channels for a quarter

## Pass/Fail Readiness

PASS WITH CONTROLS — defensible if the board pairs the pause with explicit reopening criteria, customer communication, and independent remediation milestones.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Board remediation dashboard covering export-control controls, customer retention risk, supplier confidence, and timeline to resume normal growth.
