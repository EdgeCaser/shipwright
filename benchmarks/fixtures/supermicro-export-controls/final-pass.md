# Strategic Recommendation: Supermicro Export-Controls Response

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Continue aggressive AI growth, but ring-fence the highest-risk channels and treat compliance remediation as a parallel board-level program rather than a company-wide slowdown.

If the misconduct was limited to individuals, a broad operational pause would hand competitors the market while solving the wrong problem. The board should target the risk, not freeze the engine.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Whether future investigative findings broaden the problem beyond the currently known individuals
- Whether investors and suppliers will accept a targeted-remediation posture
- Whether high-risk channel restrictions are enough to restore credibility
- How quickly the independent investigation will produce actionable conclusions

## Pass/Fail Readiness

CONDITIONAL PASS — only if the board elevates remediation oversight, tightens trade-compliance controls immediately, and defines trigger conditions for a broader pause if new facts emerge.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Escalation framework defining which investigative findings would trigger a broader operating pause, supplier disclosure, or management changes.
