# Strategic Recommendation: Meta Muse Spark Frontier Strategy

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Keep Muse-family frontier models closed for the next 2 release cycles while explicitly preserving an open-weight path for smaller follow-on models.

Meta does not need to win the open-source identity contest right now; it needs to prove it can ship product-integrated frontier systems that matter. Muse Spark is the first credible sign that the reset is working.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Independent benchmark validation is still thin
- We do not yet know whether developers will materially punish Meta for closing the frontier tier
- Product monetization impact across Meta surfaces is still unproven
- The timing and scope of any future open release are unresolved

## Pass/Fail Readiness

CONDITIONAL PASS — viable if Meta pairs the closed strategy with explicit milestones for product adoption, partner uptake, and a decision checkpoint on reopening the roadmap.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Three-path strategy memo comparing: closed frontier / hybrid release ladder / full return to open weights, with ecosystem and monetization impacts.
