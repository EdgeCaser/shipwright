# Strategic Recommendation: Yahoo-Microsoft Acquisition

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Reopen negotiations with Microsoft. Accept a revised offer in the $35-37/share range.

Yahoo's independent strategy depends on display advertising dominance and the Google search partnership. Display is being fragmented by Facebook's explosive growth. The Google partnership faces DOJ antitrust review and likely won't survive. Yahoo Search at 20% share and declining cannot compete independently against Google's 63%.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Whether Microsoft would return with a higher bid after being rejected
- DOJ outcome on Google-Yahoo search partnership
- Alibaba and Yahoo Japan stake valuations if held long-term
- Whether Yahoo's display advertising moat survives the social media shift

## Pass/Fail Readiness

CONDITIONAL PASS — reopen talks with a counteroffer at $37/share. The $33 offer represented a 62% premium; anything above $35 delivers exceptional shareholder value against Yahoo's declining competitive position.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Negotiation strategy memo: acceptable price range, structural preferences (all-cash vs. stock), and break-up fee provisions.
