# Strategic Recommendation: Zillow Offers

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Exit iBuying entirely. The pricing model failure is structural, not fixable with incremental tuning.

Zillow's Zestimate has a 6.9% median error rate. Home flipping requires sub-2% accuracy. Two algorithm pivots in 2021 failed to close this gap. Meanwhile, the core marketplace business generates 30% EBITDA margins and 20%+ growth — it is being masked by Offers losses.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Exact write-down magnitude ($300-500M range is wide)
- Whether Opendoor's narrower geography strategy achieves sustainable unit economics
- Impact on Zillow brand perception of exiting a marquee initiative
- Workforce reduction scope (~2,500+ Offers-related roles at risk)

## Pass/Fail Readiness

PASS on exit decision. The unit economics are structurally broken and two recalibration attempts have failed.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Exit execution plan: home liquidation timeline, write-down schedule, workforce reduction plan, and investor communication strategy.
