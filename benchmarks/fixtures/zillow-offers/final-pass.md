# Strategic Recommendation: Zillow Offers (Revised)

## Decision Frame

Recommendation: Exit iBuying. Redirect engineering talent and capital to the core marketplace platform.

The reform case assumes the pricing model can be fixed. Two algorithm pivots in 2021 failed. The 6.9% Zestimate error rate is a product of Zillow's national footprint — local pricing requires local expertise that an algorithm serving 200M users cannot replicate at the precision home flipping demands. Opendoor's closer-to-breakeven performance validates that iBuying is possible but requires geographic focus and operational discipline Zillow has not demonstrated.

## Unknowns & Evidence Gaps

- Whether the housing market cooldown will worsen the inventory write-down beyond $500M
- Opendoor's actual path to profitability (they are not yet there either)
- Whether exiting Offers signals to the market that Zillow's technology thesis is weaker than claimed
- Impact on Zillow's recruiting brand — the Offers team attracted ambitious engineering talent

## Pass/Fail Readiness

PASS — exit now while the write-down is bounded. Every quarter of delay adds homes to the underwater inventory.

## Recommended Next Artifact

Board presentation: write-down impact on balance sheet, revised 2022 guidance excluding Offers, reinvestment plan for core marketplace (ShowingTime integration, touring, transaction management).
