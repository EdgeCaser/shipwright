# /strategy, Product Strategy Workflow

Run this command to develop or refresh a product strategy through a structured workshop format.

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Establish Strategic Context
Read and apply `/skills/strategy/product-strategy-session/SKILL.md`.

Gather context:
- Check CLAUDE.md for existing product context
- Ask the PM about: product stage, current metrics, biggest win, biggest challenge
- Review any discovery-researcher outputs for market and customer context

### Step 2: Articulate the Vision
Draft or refine the product vision:
- Who is it for?
- What's the aspirational future state?
- How is it differentiated?

Validate: Is it inspirational, directional, customer-centric, and durable (3-5 years)?

### Step 3: Define Strategic Bets
For each bet (2-4 maximum):
- Thesis: "We believe [X] will [Y] because [Z]"
- What would have to be true (key assumptions)
- Investment level (Major / Moderate / Exploratory)
- Success metric
- Kill criteria

### Step 4: Set Boundaries
Define what the team is NOT doing:
- Conscious "no" decisions with rationale
- Deferred items with revisit triggers

### Step 5: Run a Pre-Mortem
Read and apply `/skills/execution/pre-mortem/SKILL.md`.

Imagine the strategy failed:
- What went wrong? (customer, execution, market, technical failure modes)
- Prioritize risks by likelihood × impact
- Define mitigations for top 3 risks

### Step 6: Define Success Criteria
For each bet:
- Leading indicator (check at 4 weeks)
- Lagging indicator (check at quarter-end)
- Review cadence (weekly, monthly, quarterly)

## Output

Produce a **Product Strategy Document** containing:
1. Product Vision
2. Strategic Context (current state, market, customer)
3. Strategic Bets (2-4, with theses and kill criteria)
4. Strategic Boundaries (what we're NOT doing)
5. Pre-Mortem Summary (top risks and mitigations)
6. Success Criteria & Review Cadence
