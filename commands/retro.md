# /retro - Retrospective Workflow

Run this command to facilitate a structured team retrospective and turn observations into committed actions.

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Choose Format
Read and apply the framework from `/skills/measurement/retrospective-facilitator/SKILL.md`.

Ask the PM:
- What are we retro-ing? (sprint, launch, incident, quarter)
- What format fits? (Start/Stop/Continue, 4Ls, Sailboat, Mad/Sad/Glad)
- Who participated and what's the mood? (celebratory, frustrated, neutral)

If unsure, default to Start/Stop/Continue for sprints and 4Ls for larger milestones.

### Step 2: Gather Observations
Ask the PM to share observations or paste raw notes. Organize them into the chosen framework's categories. Group related items and note frequency (how many people raised the same point).

### Step 3: Identify Patterns
Look across the observations for:
- Recurring themes (things that keep showing up retro after retro)
- Root causes (not just symptoms)
- Quick wins vs. systemic issues
- What went well that should be protected or amplified

### Step 4: Commit to Actions
For the top 3-5 themes, produce specific actions:
- What exactly will change
- Who owns it
- By when
- How we'll know it worked

Keep the list short. Five committed actions beat fifteen forgotten ones.

## Output

Produce a **Retrospective Summary** containing:
1. Observations organized by framework category
2. Top themes with frequency counts
3. Action items with owners and deadlines
4. One thing to celebrate and carry forward
